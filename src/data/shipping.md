# Shipping Policy

## Domestic Shipping
- **Processing Time:** 1-2 business days.
- **Delivery Time:** 3-5 business days for metro cities, 5-7 days for other locations.
- **Charges:** Free shipping on orders above ₹2000. Flat rate of ₹100 for orders below ₹2000.

## International Shipping
- **Processing Time:** 2-3 business days.
- **Delivery Time:** 7-14 business days.
- **Customs & Duties:** Customers are responsible for any customs duties or taxes applicable in their country.

## Tracking
Tracking details will be sent to your registered email or phone number once the order is dispatched.
