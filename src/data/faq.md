# Frequently Asked Questions

## Orders & Shipping

### How long does shipping take?
We typically process orders within 24-48 hours. Shipping takes 3-5 business days for domestic orders and 7-14 business days for international orders.

### Can I track my order?
Yes, once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email.

### Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we ship worldwide! Shipping costs will be calculated at checkout based on your location.

## Returns & Exchanges

### What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unworn and in their original packaging.

### How do I initiate a return?
Please visit our [Returns Center](/returns) or contact our support team at support@example.com.

## Product Care

### How do I care for my jewelry?
- Avoid direct contact with perfumes and lotions.
- Store in a cool, dry place.
- Clean with a soft cloth.

### Is your gold hallmarked?
Yes, all our gold jewelry is BIS hallmarked for purity.
